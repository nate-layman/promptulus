# The AI Literacy Companions

A steampunk-inspired cast designed to teach fundamental AI skills through personality and metaphor. Each character teaches one skill. Together they form a complete curriculum framed around the AI context window — your AI's "page limit."

The landing page mascot, **Octavius the Octopus**, holds all 8 skills together with its 8 arms.

---

## Skill Framework: Before, During, After

### Before AI — Planning (2 skills)
Skills you use before you touch an AI tool.

### During AI — i^4 (4 skills)
Four things that compete for the same finite context window space:
- **Intent** — your goals and boundaries
- **Instructions** — your prompt
- **Information** — your documents and data
- **Interaction** — your conversation history

### After AI — Quality (2 skills)
Skills for evaluating and documenting what AI produced.

## World Theme
A **steampunk academy of cognition**, where each mentor teaches a facet of thinking with AI. The curriculum follows the natural workflow: plan before, execute during, quality-check after. The unifying concept is the context window — AI's finite "page limit" that all skills must work within.
